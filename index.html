How It Works

Donate Surplus Food

Events and households list extra food with location details. Our smart matching algorithm finds the best NGO partners nearby.

NGO Coordinates Pickup

Verified NGOs with high reliability scores receive notifications and coordinate fast, safe pickup within the time window.

Delivered to Recipients

Nutritious meals reach orphanages, old-age homes, and community kitchens on time, tracked for impact measurement.

Platform Features

Location-Based Matching

GPS-powered proximity matching ensures NGOs closest to surplus food get priority notifications

Reliability Scoring

NGOs are rated on completion rates and on-time delivery, ensuring quality service

Real-Time Tracking

Track donation status from submission to delivery with live updates

Instant Notifications

NGOs receive immediate alerts when matching donations become available nearby

Safe & Verified

All NGO partners are verified with registration numbers and location confirmation

SDG Aligned Impact

Track contributions to UN SDG 2 (Zero Hunger), SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption), and SDG 17 (Partnerships)
